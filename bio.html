INTRODUCTION

Taika Waititi is a filmmaker, actor and comedian, born in New Zealand. He is well known for directing the superheroe films: Thor: Rangarok (2017), Thor: Love and Thunder (2022) and the black comedy: Jojo Rabbit (2019). During his career, he has been awarded with an Academy Award, BAFTA Award and Grammy Award. In addition to his director career, he has starred in diferent films as an actor, co-director and producer.

EARLY YEARS

He was born on the 16th August, 1975. He studied theatre at Victoria University of Wellington. While he was a student, he was part of a five-member comedy group that travelled around New Zealand doing shows. In 2003, he directed his first short called Two Cars, One Night (2003) that earned him an Academy Award nomination in 2005. Boy (2010) was his first film. It was a comedy-drama written and directed by him. It premiered at the Sundance Film Festival.

It was in 2013 when he started to have the worldwide recognizement, he wrote, and acted on the mockumentary What We Do In The Shadows that in 2019 was launched as a series for FX. The series was awarded with an Emmy for Outstanding comedy Series.

WORLWIDE RECOGNIZEMENT

In 2016 he commenced working with disney, he wrote the inicial play for the animation movie Moana but the approach of the movie changed in the final product. In 2017, he directed his biggest movie, Thor: Ragnarok. As it is a Disney, Marvel, movie the worldwide recognizement was huge and he earned critical praise. Another of his most famous movies was Jojo Rabbit (2019), it was nominated for two Academy Awards for Best Picture and won Best Adapted Screenplay. He continued with his contract with Disney and participated in the series The Mandalorian directing one of the episodes

LATEST CAREER

Related with his latest works, he portrayed Ratcatcher in the DC superhero movie The Suicide Squad , the antagonist in the comedy movie Free Guy and co-create the comedy series Reservation Dogs. He worked with the streaming platform HBO producing, directing and starring in the comedy series Our Flag Means Death. For the other big streaming platform he wrote and directed the animated series, Charlie and The Chocolate Factory and Thor: Love and Thunder has been her latest most famous movie.
